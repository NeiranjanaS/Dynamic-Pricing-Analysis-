# Business Recommendations

## Revenue Suggestions

- Implement dynamic pricing during peak seasons to maximize revenue.
- Offer promotional discounts during low-demand periods to increase occupancy.
- Monitor Average Daily Rate (ADR) regularly to optimize pricing.

---

## Customer Retention Suggestions

- Identify customers with high cancellation risk and send reminder emails.
- Encourage repeat bookings through loyalty programs and special offers.
- Promote non-refundable booking options with attractive discounts.

---

## Pricing Suggestions

- Adjust room prices based on seasonal demand and booking trends.
- Increase prices during holidays and high-demand months.
- Offer competitive pricing for early bookings and last-minute deals.

---

## Expected Business Impact

- Reduced booking cancellations
- Increased hotel occupancy
- Improved customer retention
- Higher overall revenue through optimized pricing
