# SQL Insights

## Booking Summary

- Total bookings were analyzed from the cleaned hotel booking dataset.
- City Hotels received more bookings than Resort Hotels.

## Cancellation Analysis

- Online Travel Agency bookings had a higher cancellation rate.
- Bookings with Non-Refund deposits showed the lowest cancellation rate.
- Longer lead times generally resulted in higher cancellation probability.

## Pricing Analysis

- Average Daily Rate (ADR) varied across hotel types.
- Peak season months showed significantly higher ADR values.

## Seasonal Trends

- July and August recorded the highest booking volume.
- January and February experienced comparatively lower demand.

## Business Recommendations

- Introduce dynamic pricing during high-demand months.
- Encourage non-refundable deposits during peak seasons.
- Target customers with long lead times using reminder emails and promotional offers.
- Focus marketing campaigns on high-cancellation customer segments.
